Products & Services

At Thabi’s Sausage and Grill, we pride ourselves in offering a variety of delicious and pork-free food products:

  • Burgers
  • Kota’s
  • Boerewors Rolls
  • Fries
  • Pap n Vleis (only served as a special)
  • Dagwood
  • Vegan Special (available on Thursdays)

All our foods are completely pork-free, ensuring that everyone can enjoy our meals safely and comfortably. We highly recommend our services to customers of all backgrounds.

We have proudly catered to various events, ensuring top-quality service for each customer. Whether it’s at school sports events, public gatherings, or corporate functions, our team is always ready to serve.

Currently, you can find us at Varsity College and Menlyn Mall in Pretoria, operating daily to bring you the best in grilled foods.
