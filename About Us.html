About Thabi's Sausage and Grill

Our Story

In the food industry, Thabi's Sausage and Grill was founded with the goal of turning a profit, but more importantly, to enhance the lives of our clients and improve relationships between vendors and well-established companies.

We pride ourselves on maintaining a clean atmosphere while being known for our exceptional services in and around Pretoria. Many choose us because of our reliability and quality service.

Our Mission

To provide quality food to the community in a convenient and affordable way to enhance our customers' lives.

Our Philosophy and Vision

Our philosophy is simple: "Don’t stop when you’re tired, stop when you’re done." Entrepreneurship comes with its challenges, but persistence is the key to success, especially in the food industry. Our vision is to become the most recognized food truck business in South Africa.

Our Goals

We aim to make our customers happy and grow the business rapidly. Our goal is to be recognized on a national scale while ensuring consistent customer satisfaction.

Products & Services

Thabi’s Sausage and Grill offers a wide range of food products, including:

  • Burgers
  • Kota’s
  • Boerewors Rolls
  • Fries
  • Pap n Vleis (only served as a special)
  • Dagwood
  • Vegan Special (on Thursday)

All our foods are pork-free, and we proudly offer catering services for events such as sports festivals, DA conferences, Bikers festivals, and more.

Our Presence

We are currently located at Varsity College and Menlyn Mall, running our food business daily. We have also catered for various events such as birthday parties, weddings, and corporate functions.

We are also working on expanding our services through platforms like Uber Eats and MrD to make our food more accessible to our customers.
